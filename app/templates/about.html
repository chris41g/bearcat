About Sidney

Network Scanner & Discovery Tool

Sidney is a powerful tool designed to help network administrators discover and catalog devices on their networks. With Sidney, you can perform comprehensive scans of your network infrastructure to identify online hosts, running services, and system information.

Features
  • Network scanning and host discovery
  • Service and port scanning
  • Operating system detection
  • Windows system information gathering
  • Share enumeration
  • Data persistence and querying
